Everyone loves food! But food isn’t the only aspect of a restaurant experience. Predicting what each patron would like or how their experience can be best catered is a challenging task. If done poorly, could leave the patron with an inadequate experience; and you, with a scathing review. Can technology help?

We propose designing a co-vigilant system that will accurately detect, predict, and signal any adverse event in the restaurant’s social media outlook. Restaurateurs will then be vigilant in resolving any issues before they even occur!
